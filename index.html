Mission Statement

Eat, Drink, Give Thanks, and provide special gifts to children diagnosed with cancer or chronic blood diseases.


SunningHill Jill Kids is a 501(c) non-profit organization, all donations are tax deductible. 19825-B North Cove Road #141, Cornelius, NC


Where Does Your Donation Go?

Your donations impact these children and their families NOW. SunningHill Jill Kids provides a minimum of 30 iPads per month to Levine Children's Hospital Hematology and Oncology Center as well as approximatley 200 books and 20 Family Gift Bags each month....

Did You Know?

In 2016 Levine Children’s Hospital was nationally ranked by U.S. News and World Reports as one of the "Best Children’s Hospital" in 6 Specialties including Cancer Treatment.
