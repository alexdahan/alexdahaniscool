SunningHill Jill Kids is a non profit 501(c) 3 charity that raises funds through donations and cooking class parties. These donations are used for iPads and other special gifts for patients and their families at Levine Children's Hospital undergoing treatment and hospitalization for cancer and chronic blood disorders. Our hope at SunningHill Jill Kids is that these gifts will allow moments of joy and discovery, and give time away from daily challenges.
